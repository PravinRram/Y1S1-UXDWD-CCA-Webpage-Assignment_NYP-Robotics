about us

We are NYP Robotics -- builders, programmers, and innovators.

Who We Are

Founded in 2023 by Pravin and Sam, NYP Robotics is a student-led club for robotics enthusiasts, united with a shared passion for building robots and solving real-world problems, to collaborate on hands-on projects, hone technical skills, and push the boundaries of what's possible in recreational robotics.

Collaborative & Inclusive

Eversyone is welcomed here; whether you're a freshman exploring an interest in robotics, or a senior with years of experience in several proficiencies. We promote collaboration between various ability levels so everyone can contribute and grow, and make lifelong friends along the way!

Hands-On Experience

We believe that the best way to master robotics is by building robots yourself, so we provide many opportunities to do so -- weekly workshops, peer-led tutorials, and code reviews ensure every member gains real skills in mechanics, electronics, and coding.

Competitive Spirit

We put our skills to the test in national and international competitions, where specialised members in certain areas of robotics can participate under intense conditions to emerge victorious while representing our club.

Driven by Innovation & Impact

Beyond competitions, we aim to use our robotics skills to address real life needs; like making an assistive device for everyday tasks. We collaborate with community organisations as volunteers to make robotics beneficial to Singapore.

Mission & Vision

Mission >

To empower students with hands-on experience in robotics by fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and innovative environment where ideas come to life. We aim to cultivate technical skills, creative problem-solving, and teamwork through real-world projects, competitions, and learning opportunities.

To expand horizons for students who explore robotics recreationally — where every student, regardless of background, has the opportunity to explore, build, and contribute to a smarter, more connected Singapore.

Achiev­ements

  • Represented NYP

    Singapore National VEX V5 Robotics Championship 2024

    Designed and built VEX robots to move using controls and autonomously through an obstacle course.

  • 1st Runner-Up

    National Robotics Competition 2023 - Regular Category, Tertiary Division

    Recognised for Best Programming and Engineering Design out of over 50 polytechnic teams across Singapore.

  • Top 10 Finalist

    Singapore Amazing Flying Machine Competition (SAFMC) 2024 - Category D2 (Multi-Machine)

    Designed and built autonomous drones to perform tasks in an obstacle course.

  • Lego Smart Assistive Arm Prosthetic Protoype

    NYP Innovation Fair 2025

    Built an arm prosthetic using Lego and showcased it at the innovation fair.

  • Micro:bit Teaching Session

    Volunteering at Lorem Secondary School 2023

    Taught Robotics using the Micro:bit platform to secondary 2 students.
